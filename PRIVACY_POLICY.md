# Privacy Policy for NeonDrop Blocks
**Last updated: 29.11.2025**

This Privacy Policy explains how **NeonDrop Blocks** ("the App", "we", "us", or "our") handles user information.  
NeonDrop Blocks is a standalone puzzle game that operates entirely offline.  
We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**  
NeonDrop Blocks does not require account creation, login, or personal information of any kind.

The App does **not** collect:
- Name  
- Email  
- Phone number  
- Location data  
- Payment information  
- Device identifiers  
- Advertising identifiers (IDFA)  
- Usage analytics  
- Tracking data  

The App stores only local gameplay data such as:
- High scores  
- Difficulty settings  
- User preferences (sound on/off)

These values are stored **locally on the user's device** and are never transmitted to external servers.

---

## 2. Third-Party Services

The App does **not** use:
- Analytics tools  
- Advertising SDKs  
- Tracking technologies  
- Third-party data processors  
- Cloud-based services  

All data processing is performed locally on the user’s device.

---

## 3. Data Storage and Security

Since we do not collect personal data, we do not store any user data on external servers.

Locally stored settings and high scores remain entirely under the user's control.  
When the App is deleted, all stored data is permanently removed.

---

## 4. Children’s Privacy

NeonDrop Blocks is suitable for all ages.  
Because no personal information is collected, the App satisfies privacy requirements for children's apps.

---

## 5. Your Rights

As the App does not collect or store personal information, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete outside of what is stored locally on your device.

Reinstalling or deleting the App will reset all stored data automatically.

---

## 6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as needed to reflect changes in features or legal requirements.  
Updates will be posted on this page with the latest "Last updated" date.
